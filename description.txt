<h3 id="item-description__teamdream-responsive-team-card-bootstrap-template">TeamDream v2.0 — Responsive Team Card Bootstrap Template</h3>
<p><strong>TeamDream v2.0</strong> is a premium HTML team card template featuring <strong>10 unique, fully distinct layouts</strong> — from classic grids and editorial dark cards to glassmorphism, overlay hover, and interactive department filters. Every layout is pixel-perfect, W3C valid, and ready to drop into any website with compiled CSS included.</p>

<p>Built from the ground up with <strong>Bootstrap 5.3.3</strong>, <strong>Sass source files</strong>, <strong>CSS custom properties</strong> for effortless dark mode, and <strong>vanilla JS</strong> for filtering — no jQuery, just clean and modern code. The included <code>package.json</code> is optional and only needed if you want to rebuild CSS from SCSS.</p>

<img src="https://teamdream.netlify.app/src/assets/img/rate.png" alt="rate-us">

<h3 id="item-description__10-layouts">10 Unique Layouts Included:</h3>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Layout 01 — Classic Grid Light:</strong> 4-column portrait card grid on a warm neutral background. Ghost social icon circles, lift-on-hover shadow.</li>
    <li><strong>Layout 02 — Editorial Dark Grid:</strong> 3-column landscape photo cards on a deep navy gradient. Left-aligned typography with an indigo accent rule.</li>
    <li><strong>Layout 03 — Horizontal Cards:</strong> Full-width row cards with photo left, bio right. Accent border slides in from the left on hover.</li>
    <li><strong>Layout 04 — Full Overlay Hover:</strong> Portrait cards where hovering reveals name, role, bio and social links via a smooth dark overlay.</li>
    <li><strong>Layout 05 — Minimal Circle:</strong> Ultra-minimal layout with circular photos — a floating card materialises on hover.</li>
    <li><strong>Layout 06 — Glassmorphism:</strong> Frosted glass cards over a vivid purple gradient. Blur + saturation backdrop filter with graceful fallback.</li>
    <li><strong>Layout 07 — List View:</strong> Team directory in a filterable table format with avatar, badge, socials, and profile link per row.</li>
    <li><strong>Layout 08 — Skills &amp; Tags Dark:</strong> Dark mode cards with square-cropped photos, skill pill badges, and experience indicators.</li>
    <li><strong>Layout 09 — Featured + Grid:</strong> Asymmetric layout — large featured leader card on the left, compact 3-column mini-grid on the right.</li>
    <li><strong>Layout 10 — Filter by Department:</strong> Square-crop cards with coloured department badges. Instant filter tabs for Leadership, Engineering, Design, Marketing.</li>
</ul>
